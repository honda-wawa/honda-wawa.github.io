Honda B-Series Transmission Ratios

How to import this data into Google Sheets:
  1. Open Google Sheets
  2. Create a new spreadsheet
  3. In cell A1, paste this formula:
  4. =IMPORTHTML("https://honda-wawa.github.io/honda_bseries_transmissions.html", "table", 1)
  5. Press Enter and the data will automatically load!
